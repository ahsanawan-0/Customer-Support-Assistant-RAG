# Trailhead Outfitters — Frequently Asked Questions

## Shipping

### How much does shipping cost?
Standard shipping is a flat $6.95 and is **free on all orders over $75**. Expedited (2-day) shipping is $17.95, and Overnight is $29.95. Rates are the same across the contiguous United States.

### How long does delivery take?
Standard shipping arrives in 5–7 business days. Expedited arrives in 2 business days, and Overnight arrives the next business day when ordered before 12:00 PM MT. Orders are processed within 1–2 business days before they ship.

### Do you ship internationally?
We currently ship to the United States and Canada. Canadian orders take 7–12 business days and may be subject to import duties collected at delivery. We do not yet ship to other countries.

### How do I track my order?
Once your order ships you'll receive an email with a tracking number and a carrier link. You can also ask this chat assistant to look up your order status using your order number or the email on the order.

### My tracking hasn't updated — what should I do?
Carriers sometimes take 24–48 hours to scan a package after we hand it off. If tracking hasn't moved in more than 3 business days, contact us and we'll open an investigation with the carrier.

## Orders

### Can I change or cancel my order?
Orders can be changed or cancelled within **60 minutes** of being placed, as long as they haven't entered processing. After that we usually can't stop the order, but you can return it once it arrives. Reach out as soon as possible and we'll do our best.

### Can I add an item to an existing order?
We can't add items to an order once it's placed. Please place a separate order — remember shipping is free over $75.

### I received the wrong item. What now?
We're sorry about that. Keep the item and packaging, and start a return/exchange through this assistant or email support. We'll send the correct item right away and cover return shipping.

### An item is missing from my package.
Multi-item orders occasionally ship in separate boxes that arrive on different days. Check your shipping confirmation for multiple tracking numbers. If everything shows delivered but an item is still missing, contact us within 14 days.

## Products & Sizing

### How do I find the right size?
Every product page has a size chart. Our apparel runs true to size. For layering pieces like the Summit Ridge Jacket, size up if you plan to wear heavy insulation underneath. Boots tend to run a half size large — we recommend hiking socks when trying them on.

### Are your rain jackets actually waterproof?
Products listed as "waterproof" use fully taped/sealed seams and a waterproof membrane or coating (not just water-resistant). This includes the Cascade Rain Shell, the Summit Ridge Jacket's outer shell, and the Bedrock Boots.

### Do you offer gear repair?
Yes. Minor repairs (zippers, seams, buckles) on Trailhead-branded products are free within the warranty period. Ask the assistant to connect you with a human agent to arrange a repair.

### Is your down responsibly sourced?
Yes. The down in the Emberlite Down Hoody is certified to the Responsible Down Standard (RDS).

## Account & Payment

### What payment methods do you accept?
We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and Shop Pay. Gift cards can be applied at checkout.

### Do you have a rewards program?
Yes — Trailhead Trail Points. You earn 1 point per $1 spent, and 100 points equals $5 off. Sign up is free at checkout.

### Do you offer discounts?
New subscribers to our newsletter get 10% off their first order. We also offer a verified discount for active military, veterans, and first responders.
