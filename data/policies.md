# Trailhead Outfitters — Policies

## Returns & Exchanges Policy

### Return window
We accept returns and exchanges within **60 days** of the delivery date. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original condition with tags attached. Gently used footwear can be returned within 30 days if you're not satisfied with the fit.

### How returns work
1. Start the return through this assistant or at trailheadoutfitters.example/returns using your order number and email.
2. We email you a **prepaid return shipping label**. Returns are free within the U.S.
3. Pack the item securely (original packaging preferred) and drop it off with the carrier.
4. Once we receive and inspect the item (2–3 business days after it arrives), we issue your refund or ship your exchange.

### Refunds
Refunds go back to the original payment method and take 5–7 business days to appear after we process the return. Original expedited/overnight shipping charges are non-refundable. If you received free shipping and your return drops the order below $75, we do not retroactively charge shipping.

### Exchanges
The fastest way to exchange for a different size or color is to start an exchange in the returns flow — we place a new order for the replacement at no extra shipping cost and refund the returned item once it arrives. If the replacement is out of stock, we'll offer a backorder or a refund.

### Non-returnable items
Gift cards, final-sale/clearance items (marked at checkout), and used personal items like worn socks or opened water-filtration gear cannot be returned for hygiene and safety reasons. Damaged or defective items are always covered — see the warranty policy.

### Return shipping costs
Standard returns and exchanges within the U.S. are free with our prepaid label. For Canadian returns, the customer covers return shipping unless the item was defective or we made an error.

## Shipping Policy

- Standard shipping: $6.95, free over $75 (5–7 business days).
- Expedited: $17.95 (2 business days).
- Overnight: $29.95 (next business day if ordered before 12:00 PM MT).
- Orders are processed and dispatched within 1–2 business days.
- We ship to the U.S. and Canada. Canadian orders may incur import duties.
- We are not responsible for carrier delays due to weather, but we'll help you file a claim.

## Warranty Policy

### Trailhead Limited Warranty
All Trailhead-branded gear carries a **2-year limited warranty** against defects in materials and workmanship. This does not cover normal wear and tear, misuse, or accidental damage.

### What's covered
Seam failures, delamination, zipper or buckle defects, down leakage beyond normal, and premature sole separation on footwear. We will repair, replace, or refund defective items at our discretion.

### How to file a warranty claim
Ask this assistant to connect you with a human agent, or email warranty@trailheadoutfitters.example with your order number and photos of the issue. Most claims are reviewed within 3 business days.

## Price Match Policy
If you find an identical in-stock item advertised for less by an authorized retailer within 14 days of your purchase, we'll match the price and refund the difference. Contact a human agent with a link to the competitor listing.

## Privacy & Data
We only use your order and contact details to fulfill orders and provide support. We never sell personal information. This chat assistant can look up order details but never displays full payment card numbers.
